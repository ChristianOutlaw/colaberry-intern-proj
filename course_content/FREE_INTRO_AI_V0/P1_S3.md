# P1_S3 — AI in the Real World

## Summary

Surveys practical AI applications across industries (healthcare, retail, finance)
with short real-world examples.

## Learning Objectives

- Describe how AI is used in at least three different industries.
- Distinguish between AI that helps humans and AI that replaces tasks.
- Recognize an AI use case they could apply in their own career.

## Outline

- Healthcare: diagnostic imaging, drug discovery timelines
- Retail: demand forecasting, personalized recommendations
- Finance: fraud detection, credit scoring
- HR / recruiting: resume screening (with bias caveats)
- Cross-industry theme: AI as pattern-matching at scale
- Ethical note: transparency, fairness, human oversight
