# P1_S1 — What Is AI?

## Summary

Introduces artificial intelligence: what it is, where it appears in everyday life,
and why it matters now.

## Learning Objectives

- Define artificial intelligence in plain language.
- Identify at least three places AI appears in daily life.
- Explain why AI has become prominent in recent years.

## Outline

- Definition: AI vs. automation vs. traditional software
- Brief history: rule-based systems → machine learning → deep learning
- Everyday examples: recommendations, search, voice assistants, maps
- Why now: data availability, compute cost, open tooling
- Common misconceptions (AI is not magic, not sentient)
